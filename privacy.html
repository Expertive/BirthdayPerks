Privacy Policy — BirthdayPerks: Birthday Flow

Last updated: May 16, 2026

BirthdayPerks: Birthday Flow ("the App") is a native Shopify app that operates entirely within the Shopify platform.

Data Collection & Storage

This App does not collect, store, or process any data outside of Shopify's infrastructure.

All customer data — including birthday date, opt-in status, and discount history — is stored exclusively as Shopify customer metafields within your own Shopify store. No data is transmitted to external servers or third-party services.

How It Works

All data processing is handled natively by Shopify:

  • Metafields store customer birthday preferences
  • Shopify Flow automates discount eligibility checks
  • Shopify Discounts issues birthday discount codes

The App acts solely as a configuration layer on top of Shopify's native features.

Customer Rights (GDPR)

Customers may request access to or deletion of their personal data by contacting the store owner. Data can be removed directly from the customer's profile in Shopify Admin.

Merchant Rights

Merchants may uninstall the App at any time. Since no data is stored outside of Shopify, uninstallation has no impact on data residency.
