🎂 Birthday Discount Automation

Set up BirthdayPerks so customers can enter their birthday and automatically receive a birthday discount.

✅ Prerequisites

1
App is installed on your Shopify store.
2
New Customer Accounts is enabled in Settings → Customer accounts → Use new customer accounts.
3
Shopify Flow is installed.

🚀 Step 1: Add the Birthday Block to Customer Profile Page

4
Go to Online Store → Themes.
5
Click Customize on your active theme.
6
Navigate to Checkout and customer accounts → Profile.
7
In the left panel, click Add block under the Apps section.
8
Select BirthdayPerks - Birthday Flow.
9
Click Save.

Customers will now see a birthday date input and opt-in toggle on their profile page.

⚙️ Step 2: Configure and Activate the Flow

10
Go to Apps → Flow.
11
Open the Birthday Discount Eligibility Check workflow.
12
Click the Scheduled time trigger and set your preferred daily run time, for example 9:00 AM.
13
Optional: update the discount percentage in the Create discount step. The default value is 20%.
14
Click Turn on to activate the workflow.

🔄 How It Works

1
Customer visits their profile page, enters their birthday date, and opts in.
2
The Flow runs automatically every day at your scheduled time.
3
On the customer's birthday, a unique personal discount code is generated and applied to their account.
4
The discount is valid for 1 day and has no minimum cart amount.
5
No manual action is needed after setup.

🏷️ Discount Details

Setting Value
Discount type Percentage
Default value 20% off
Customizable Yes — editable in Flow
Code Auto-generated, unique per customer
Validity 1 day
Minimum order None

🛠️ Troubleshooting

Customer doesn't see the birthday block: Make sure the block is added to the Profile page in the theme editor and the theme is saved.

Discount not issued: Confirm the Flow is turned on and a scheduled time is set.

Customer opted in but no discount: Check that the birthday date is saved correctly on the customer profile in Admin → Customers → [customer] → Metafields.
